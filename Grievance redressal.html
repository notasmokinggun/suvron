Grievance redressal

If something's wrong, here's exactly what happens next

Start with our support team. If your issue isn't resolved there, it moves up a defined chain instead of disappearing into a queue.

For quick support, write to support@suvron.com. If it's still unresolved, escalate to our Grievance Redressal Officer at gro@suvron.com.

The escalation matrix

Level Channel Responsible officer Response time If unresolved
1 WhatsApp, email (connect@suvron.com), voice support Customer support team Within 24 hours Escalates to the Grievance Officer
2 Email or written complaint to gro@suvron.com Grievance Redressal Officer Within 7 working days Escalates to the Nodal Officer
3 Direct escalation by email or letter Nodal Officer (appointed under RBI CEPC) Within 15 working days Escalates to the NBFC partner's compliance head
4 RBI CEPC portal or Ombudsman Regulatory authority As per RBI CEPC timelines Final resolution under RBI guidelines

We're committed to RBI CEPC compliance at every level of this process, which means a defined timeline, not an indefinite wait.
