Suvron is a digital lending platform built by Shantilekh Global Technology Pvt. Ltd. Loans are sanctioned, disbursed and collected by our RBI-registered NBFC partners, who retain final control over credit approval and terms. Suvron does not itself lend money. Interest rates for loans facilitated on this platform range from 70% to 150% APR depending on tenure, amount and your credit assessment; the exact APR for your offer is disclosed in your Key Fact Statement and loan agreement before you accept.
