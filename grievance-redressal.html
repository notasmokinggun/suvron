The escalation path

Four levels, each with a named owner

Start at Level 1. If it's not resolved in that window, it moves up automatically, you don't need to chase it yourself.

Level 01

Customer support team

Reach us on WhatsApp, email at support@suvron.in, or voice support. Response within 24 hours.

Level 1
Channel WhatsApp, email, voice
Response by 24 hours
Level 02

Grievance Redressal Officer

Unresolved after Level 1? Write to gro@suvron.in. Response within 7 working days.

Level 2
Channel Email or written complaint
Response by 7 working days

Nodal Officer

Appointed under the RBI's CEPC framework. Handles direct escalation by email or letter. Response within 15 working days.
