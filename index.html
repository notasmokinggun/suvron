Good to know

Questions people ask us

Who can apply for a Suvron loan?+
Anyone between 18 and 55 with a valid PAN, Aadhaar and an active bank account can apply. You don't need an existing credit history to be considered.
How much can I borrow, and for how long?+
Loans currently go up to ₹10,000 with a tenure of up to 30 days. Your exact amount, tenure and APR depend on your eligibility check, and you'll see all three before accepting an offer.
What does a Suvron loan cost?+
APR runs between 120 and 150 percent depending on your offer. That figure and every fee are shown to you before you accept, not buried in the agreement afterward.
How fast is disbursal once I'm approved?+
Most approved loans are disbursed the same day, straight to the bank account you verify during sign-up.
Is my personal data safe with Suvron?+
Your data is encrypted and used only to process your application. It's shared with our NBFC lending partners where needed for underwriting and compliance, never sold to advertisers or unrelated third parties. See our privacy policy for the full picture.
What happens if I repay late?+
Late fees apply and are spelled out in your loan agreement before you accept. From the day a payment is missed, you'll hear from us through automated reminders and, if needed, a support agent, never a surprise call out of nowhere.
