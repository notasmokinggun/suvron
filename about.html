What we actually do

Four things Suvron is built to get right

None of this is visible from the app screen, which is the point. It should just work.

Digital KYC

Aadhaar-linked mobile OTP sign-in and PAN verification, done in one sitting on your phone.

ML-based underwriting

Our models weigh a range of data points instead of leaning on a single credit score.

Digital disbursement

Funds move straight to your bank account. Repayment and collections stay digital too.

Compliance first

RBI Fair Practices Code, the DPDP Act, and RBI's CEPC framework govern every step.

Compliance runs through every stage, not just the fine print

Fair treatment under the RBI's Fair Practices Code, data handled under the DPDP Act, and grievances resolved on a fixed timeline under the RBI's CEPC framework. If something goes wrong, there's a defined process, not a black hole.
