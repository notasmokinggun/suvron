What Suvron is

Suvron is a digital lending platform built by Shantilekh Global Technology Pvt. Ltd. We handle the technology end to end: mobile sign-in, PAN and Aadhaar verification, underwriting, disbursement, repayment and collections. The loans themselves are sanctioned and funded by our NBFC lending partners, who hold final say on credit decisions.

Instead of a single credit score, our underwriting models weigh a range of data points to judge repayment ability — the same approach most digital lenders use now, and part of why we can approve applications quickly rather than asking for a stack of paperwork.

RBI-aligned by design

Every loan is sanctioned and funded by an RBI-registered NBFC partner. Suvron builds and runs the technology; we don't lend money ourselves.

How we operate

The rules we hold ourselves to

Compliance isn't a checkbox for us — it shapes how the product is built.

Fair Practices Code

We follow the RBI's Fair Practices Code across sourcing, sanctioning and recovery — no surprise terms, no surprise calls.

DPDP-compliant data

Personal data is handled under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, with access limited to what underwriting actually needs.

CEPC grievance redressal

If something's wrong, our support team resolves it under the RBI's Complaint & Escalation Policy framework — with a named escalation path.

If a payment is missed

You'll hear from an automated reminder first, then a support agent — never a stranger

Support runs across WhatsApp, email and voice. If something's wrong with your account or a charge doesn't look right, tell us directly rather than assuming it can't be fixed.
