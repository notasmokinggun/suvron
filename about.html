About Suvron

Small loans, built with a lot of scaffolding you never see

What Suvron is

Suvron is a digital lending platform built by Shantilekh Global Technology Pvt. Ltd. We handle the technology end to end: mobile sign-in, PAN and Aadhaar verification, underwriting, disbursement, repayment and collections. The loans themselves are sanctioned and funded by our NBFC lending partners, who hold final say on credit decisions.

Instead of a single credit score, our underwriting models look at a range of data points to judge whether someone can repay a short-term loan. That's the same approach most digital lenders use now, and it's part of why we can approve applications quickly rather than asking for a stack of paperwork.

How we operate

We follow the RBI's Fair Practices Code, handle personal data under the DPDP Act, and run grievance redressal under the RBI's CEPC framework. If a payment is missed, you'll hear from an automated reminder first and, if needed, a support agent, not a surprise call from someone you've never heard of.

Support runs across WhatsApp, email and voice. If something's wrong with your account or a charge doesn't look right, we'd rather you tell us directly than give up and assume it can't be fixed.

Careers

We're building Suvron with people across engineering, data science, compliance and customer support. If you want to work on a lending product that takes both the technology and the regulation seriously, we'd like to hear from you.

Write to us at careers@suvron.com with what you'd want to work on.
